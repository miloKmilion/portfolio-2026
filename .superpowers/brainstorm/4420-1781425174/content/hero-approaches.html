Hero Geometry Approaches

Canvas 2D, mouse-reactive, generative geometry — three visual directions

A — Voronoi Tessellation

Animated Voronoi diagram with cells that warp and shift as cursor moves. Cells near the mouse glow with your primary color. Evokes cellular biology / computational geometry.

B — Organic Metaballs

Soft, blobby shapes that slowly merge and split apart. Mouse proximity causes them to attract or repel. Feels alive and playful — like watching organisms under a microscope.

C — Flowing Topology

Smooth curves that branch and reconnect like a topological map or neural pathways. Mouse creates a gravity well that bends nearby lines. Scientific and elegant.

All share

  • Canvas 2D, zero dependencies
  • Mouse-reactive with smooth easing
  • Uses your existing color palette (primary/primary-container)
  • Renders behind centered text: your name + one tagline

Tradeoffs

  • A — Most "data science", but can feel generic
  • B — Most playful/organic, but less obviously "technical"
  • C — Most elegant, but needs careful tuning to avoid looking sparse
